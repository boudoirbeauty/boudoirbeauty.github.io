SPA Pedicure and Manicures

Hard-working hands and feet can use a little rest and relaxation, and what better way to pamper and reward your feet than a luxurious spa pedicure from our Boudoir Nails and beauty bar!   Soothing hot towels, gentle massage, deep conditioning scrubs and a gorgeous new nail color will transform the way you look and feel!

Acrylic/Nail Enhancer Manicure

If your natural nails chip, break, peel, or never seem to be able to grow to a nice long length, then acrylic and gel nails offer a wonderful solution.  At Boudoir Nail bar, you will discover a variety of beautiful acrylic and gel options that will give you beautiful, low maintenance nails that are strong and durable while also remaining flexible and comfortable to wear.    Designer label and graphic stamp design nails are available in addition to our classic styles.

Waxing & Eyebrow Tinting

Boudoir Beauty offers a range of premiere day spa waxing services from face wax to body wax in our spa rooms in an efficient, professional manner. Trained in the latest techniques for waxing & hair removal, Boudoir Beauty can keep you hair free all year long. We recommend 10-14 days of hair growth for all waxing services in order to ensure the best possible result.
